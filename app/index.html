Instructions

Press a note box to hear it. Press and hold a note to select it or delete back to that note. Unmute the sound to listen as you compose.

Cantus Firmus Rules

There are a number of rules that the guide helps you follow. If you compose a complete cantus, it will turn green. To do that, you'll need to have:

  • at least 8 notes,
  • a climax (one note higher than all the others),
  • end on tonic (return to the starting note),
  • and approach the end by step (from one note above or below).

About

Created by John Leszczynski to help you learn the principles of counterpoint without spending all your time counting intervals. The program uses this counterpoint module to determine your next note choices.
